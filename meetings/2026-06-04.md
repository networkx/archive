        - current code finds total cost `5`: 
`[(1, 3), (2, None), (None, 4)],`
`[((1, 2), None), (None, (3, 4))])`
        - better solution: total cost `4`:
`[(1,3), (2,4)]`
`[((1,2), (3,4))]`
        - Dan thinks we should be able to keep all node choices while looking at edge choices. But the proof is in the POC code...
